# Welcome to Toko

Toko is your all-in-one toolkit for Revolt servers—whether you run a bustling community, a project hub, or just a close group of friends. Toko keeps things running smoothly, safely, and (most importantly) fun, so you can focus on what matters.

Packed with powerful features—admin tools, logs, games, tickets, automations, and more—Toko is always growing, with new additions shaped by your feedback.

## What Can Toko Do?

With Toko, you can:

- **Moderation:** Warnings, bans, mutes, automod, anti-spam, and more
- **Logging:** Track edits, deletes, joins, leaves, and pretty much everything else
- **Economy:** Server currency, leaderboards, shops, rewards, games
- **Tickets:** Private channels for support, suggestions, or reports *(coming soon)*
- **Fun & Games:** Mini-games, polls, interactive commands
- **Utilities:** Timers, reminders, info commands, and much more

…and that’s just the beginning! Check out the [Modules](./modules/utility) section for the full feature list.
