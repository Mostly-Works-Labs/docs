## <span id="permissions"></span>Permissions

<Info>
<strong>"Manage Server"</strong> is required to manage your server in the Toko dashboard. Additional permissions are needed for the bot to access all features.
</Info>

### **Required for Dashboard Access**

- **Manage Server** — needed to use and manage your server in the Toko dashboard.

### **Bot Permissions for Core Features**

- **View Channel** — see channels and messages
- **Read Message History** — access previous messages for context
- **Send Message** — respond and interact with users
- **Send Embeds** & **Upload Files** — share images, embeds, logs, and files
- **React** — add reactions to messages

### **Recommended for Full Bot Functionality**

- **Manage Channel**
- **Manage Role** and **Assign Roles**
- **Kick Members**, **Ban Members**, **Mute Members**, **Deafen Members**, **Timeout Members**
- **Manage Messages**
- **Manage Webhooks**
- **Masquerade**
